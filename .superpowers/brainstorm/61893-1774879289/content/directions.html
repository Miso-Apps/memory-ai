C — Warm Thought Bubble

Light warm background. Input lives in a floating white card with organic corners. Color-coded hint chips (idea=amber, meeting=blue, decision=green). Mode bar stays at the bottom but with a warm pill and emoji icons. Friendly brain emoji in the header.
