DukiAI Memory

Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 25, 2026

DukiAI Memory helps you capture text, links, voice notes, and photos. This policy explains what data we process, why we process it, and what controls you have.

1. Data We Process

  • Account data: email, authentication provider, profile information.
  • Memory content: text notes, links, voice transcriptions, and optional images you save.
  • Usage metadata: timestamps, device/app version, and feature preferences.

2. Why We Process Data

  • To provide core features such as capture, search, recall suggestions, and AI chat.
  • To improve reliability, prevent abuse, and support account recovery/security.
  • To generate optional AI summaries, categories, and insight features that you can control in settings.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We only share data with infrastructure and AI service providers required to run the product under contractual protections.

4. Your Controls

  • Export your data from Account & Privacy settings.
  • Delete selected memories or delete all memories.
  • Delete your account permanently from in-app account settings.

5. Security

We use industry-standard controls for data-in-transit and data-at-rest protections. No system is perfect, but we continuously improve our security posture.

6. Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact: support@memory-ai.app
