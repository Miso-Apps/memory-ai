✦ Your data, your rules

Privacy isn't a feature —
it's the foundation

Your memories are deeply personal. We built Memory AI with privacy at every layer, not as an afterthought.

🔐
Encrypted at rest

All your data is encrypted in our PostgreSQL database. Your thoughts are only readable by you.

🚫
Never sold

We don't sell or share your personal data with advertisers or third parties. Period.

🗑️
Delete anytime

You own your data. Delete a single memory or your entire account instantly — no waiting, no questions.

⚙️
AI is optional

Not comfortable with AI processing? Toggle off auto-summarize and auto-categorize independently per feature.
